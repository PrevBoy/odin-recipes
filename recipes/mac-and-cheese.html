Mouth watering Mac and Cheese

You will need only the most basic of ingredients to make this delicious Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 100g Cheddar cheese
  • 100g Mozzeralla cheese
  • 100g Gouda cheese
  • 500g Macaroni elbow pasta
  • 100ml Milk

Steps

  1. Boil pasta for 10 minutes. Drain pasta in colander when done boiling.
  2. In a sauce pan or pot boil 50ml milk on medium to low heat
  3. Add 50g of each cheese to warm milk. Stir continously.
  4. Add pasta to the pot and stir.
  5. Add remaining cheese and milk to the pot and stir to mix in well.
