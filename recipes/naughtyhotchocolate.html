Naughty Hot Chocolate

For those nights that are extra cold

Just takes 5 minutes to make

Ingredients

  • 300ml milk
  • 1 shot Baileys
  • 1 shot Kalhua
  • 2 sachets of Swiss Miss Hot Chocolate

Steps

  1. Pour milk into mug and heat for 2 minutes in microwave or till desired temperature
  2. Add in the hot chocolate and stir well
  3. Pour in Baileys and Kalhua and stir well
